how to make roasted salmon

ingredients

  • salmon
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • finely chopped chives

steps

  1. heat the oven to about 420°F (215°C). while it's heating up, do this:
  2. put the salmon in a pan that you lined with parchment paper
  3. salt the salmon a little bit
  4. cook it in the pre-heated oven for about 10-12 minutes
  5. check if it's flaky with a fork. if it's not, then put it back in the oven for a few minutes
  6. once it's flaky, crack black pepper over it, and put some of the chives on there
  7. let it sit for like 4 minutes so the black pepper and chives can kind of bloom with the heat of the salmon
  8. enjoy
