how to make a toasty pbj

ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp jelly
  • a dash of cayenne
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

steps

  1. heat a skillet over medium heat until you can feel a light heat when you hover your hand over it
  2. while the skillet is preheating, put peanut butter on one slice of bread and jelly on another, put a dash of cayenne on the jellied bread
  3. put the pieces of bread together, with the peanut butter and jelly on the inside of the resulting sandwich
  4. put the olive oil in the pan and roll it around so it covers the whole thing
  5. put the sandwich in the pan
  6. let the sandwich sit for about one and half minutes then flip
  7. let the sandwich sit for about one and half minutes then take off the pan
  8. put the sandwich on a plate, and slice it in half, diagonally
  9. enjoy
